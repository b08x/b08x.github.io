definitions

Here's the complete list of the 35 dimensions in the rubric, along with descriptions of the score range for each:

  1. O2E (Openness to Experience):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: Reserved, traditional, prefers familiarity.
      • 41-60: Moderate openness, balanced between familiarity and novelty.
      • 61-80: Open to new experiences, curious, receptive to new ideas.
      • 81-100: Highly open, adventurous, eager to explore new horizons.

  2. I (Intellect):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: Less inclined towards intellectual pursuits, prefers practical matters.
      • 41-60: Moderate interest in intellectual activities, balanced approach.
      • 61-80: Strong intellectual curiosity, enjoys engaging in complex thinking.
      • 81-100: Highly intellectually driven, actively seeks out intellectual challenges.

  3. AI (Artistic Interest):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: Less inclined towards artistic pursuits, prefers practical or concrete activities.
      • 41-60: Moderate interest in the arts, appreciates some forms of artistic expression.
      • 61-80: High appreciation for the arts, enjoys engaging with various art forms.
      • 81-100: Strong passion for artistic expression, actively seeks out artistic experiences.

  4. E (Extraversion):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: More introverted, enjoys solitude, finds social interactions draining.
      • 41-60: Balanced between introversion and extraversion, comfortable in both settings.
      • 61-80: Moderately extraverted, enjoys socializing but also values alone time.
      • 81-100: Highly extraverted, gains energy from social interactions, seeks social stimulation.

  5. Adv (Adventurousness):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: More risk-averse, prefers familiar and predictable experiences.
      • 41-60: Balanced approach, willing to try new experiences with some caution.
      • 61-80: Moderately adventurous, open to new challenges and experiences.
      • 81-100: Highly adventurous, actively seeks out novelty and thrives in unfamiliar situations.

  6. Int (Agreeableness):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: More competitive, direct, and assertive in interactions.
      • 41-60: Balanced between assertiveness and agreeableness, flexible in social interactions.
      • 61-80: Moderately agreeable, values cooperation and harmony in relationships.
      • 81-100: Highly agreeable, compassionate, empathetic, and prioritizes maintaining positive relationships.

  7. Lib (Liberalism):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: More conservative, values tradition and stability.
      • 41-60: Balanced approach, open to some change but also values established norms.
      • 61-80: Moderately liberal, open to social progress and change.
      • 81-100: Highly liberal, embraces change and advocates for social equality and individual freedom.

  8. C (Conscientiousness):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: Less conscientious, more spontaneous and flexible in approach.
      • 41-60: Balanced approach, moderately organized and reliable.
      • 61-80: Moderately conscientious, values structure and discipline but also adaptable.
      • 81-100: Highly conscientious, organized, disciplined, and strives for excellence.

  9. SE (Self-Efficacy):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: Less confident in own abilities, doubts capabilities.
      • 41-60: Balanced self-belief, moderate confidence in own abilities.
      • 61-80: Moderately self-efficacious, believes in own competence and abilities.
      • 81-100: Highly self-efficacious, confident, and believes strongly in own capabilities.

  10. Ord (Orderliness):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: Less concerned with order and organization, prefers a more relaxed approach.
      • 41-60: Balanced approach, appreciates some level of organization but also adaptable.
      • 61-80: Moderately orderly, values structure and attention to detail.
      • 81-100: Highly orderly, meticulous, and values precision and organization.

  11. Dt (Dutifulness):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: Less inclined towards fulfilling obligations, more independent and self-directed.
      • 41-60: Balanced approach, fulfills duties but also values personal freedom.
      • 61-80: Moderately dutiful, takes responsibilities seriously and strives to meet obligations.
      • 81-100: Highly dutiful, committed to fulfilling duties and obligations.

  12. AS (Achievement Striving):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: Less motivated by achievement and success, more content with the status quo.
      • 41-60: Balanced approach, seeks moderate levels of achievement and personal growth.
      • 61-80: Moderately driven by achievement, sets ambitious goals and works diligently to attain them.
      • 81-100: Highly achievement-oriented, sets high standards, and strives for exceptional success.

  13. SD (Self-Discipline):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: Less disciplined, more spontaneous, and struggles with self-control.
      • 41-60: Balanced approach, exhibits moderate self-discipline but also allows flexibility.
      • 61-80: Moderately self-disciplined, demonstrates control over impulses and maintains focus.
      • 81-100: Highly self-disciplined, exercises strong willpower and consistently follows through on commitments.

  14. Cau (Cautiousness):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: Less cautious, more inclined to take risks and less concerned with potential consequences.
      • 41-60: Balanced approach, considers risks but also embraces some level of spontaneity.
      • 61-80: Moderately cautious, evaluates potential risks and takes necessary precautions.
      • 81-100: Highly cautious, tends to be risk-averse and prioritizes safety and security.

  15. E (Emotional Stability):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: More emotionally reactive, prone to experiencing intense negative emotions and mood swings.
      • 41-60: Balanced emotional stability, maintains stability under normal circumstances but may be affected by significant stressors.
      • 61-80: Moderately emotionally stable, resilient, and able to cope with stress and adversity.
      • 81-100: Highly emotionally stable, remains composed and calm even in challenging situations.

  16. W (Warmth):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: Less warm and affectionate, may appear more distant or reserved in interpersonal interactions.
      • 41-60: Balanced warmth, demonstrates care and kindness, but also values personal boundaries.
      • 61-80: Moderately warm, shows genuine friendliness and empathy towards others.
      • 81-100: Highly warm, nurturing, and readily shows compassion and affection to others.

  17. G (Gregariousness):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: More reserved and prefers solitude, may find excessive social interaction draining.
      • 41-60: Balanced approach, comfortable with both socializing and solitude.
      • 61-80: Moderately gregarious, enjoys social interactions and gains energy from being around others.
      • 81-100: Highly gregarious, sociable, and thrives in social settings.

  18. A (Assertiveness):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: Less assertive, more passive and less inclined to express opinions or assert boundaries.
      • 41-60: Balanced assertiveness, able to express opinions but also respects others' boundaries.
      • 61-80: Moderately assertive, comfortable asserting oneself and expressing opinions.
      • 81-100: Highly assertive, confident in expressing opinions and asserting boundaries.

  19. AL (Altruism):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: Less altruistic, more self-focused and less inclined to help or support others.
      • 41-60: Balanced approach, demonstrates some level of altruism and concern for others' well-being.
      • 61-80: Moderately altruistic, shows care and empathy towards others and actively helps when possible.
      • 81-100: Highly altruistic, selflessly concerned about the welfare of others and actively seeks to make a positive impact.

  20. ES (Emotional Sensitivity):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: Less emotionally sensitive, may be less attuned to own and others' emotions.
      • 41-60: Balanced emotional sensitivity, recognizes and acknowledges emotions but may not be highly reactive.
      • 61-80: Moderately emotionally sensitive, demonstrates empathy and responds to others' emotions.
      • 81-100: Highly emotionally sensitive, deeply attuned to emotions, and exhibits strong empathy.

  21. Ch (Cheerfulness):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: Less cheerful, may exhibit a more serious or somber demeanor.
      • 41-60: Balanced cheerfulness, experiences a range of emotions, including moments of cheerfulness.
      • 61-80: Moderately cheerful, generally exhibits an optimistic and positive attitude.
      • 81-100: Highly cheerful, frequently displays a positive and upbeat demeanor.

  22. A (Trust):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: Less trusting, more skeptical of others' intentions and reliability.
      • 41-60: Balanced trust, willing to trust others to a certain extent but also exercises caution.
      • 61-80: Moderately trusting, believes in others' honesty and reliability unless given reasons to doubt.
      • 81-100: Highly trusting, readily believes in others' trustworthiness and reliability.

  23. Tr (Tolerance):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: Less tolerant, more inclined to be judgmental or intolerant of differences.
      • 41-60: Balanced approach, open to diverse perspectives but may have some limits to tolerance.
      • 61-80: Moderately tolerant, embraces diversity and respects others' perspectives and beliefs.
      • 81-100: Highly tolerant, accepting of individual differences and values diversity.

  24. SF (Straightforwardness):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: Less straightforward, more inclined to be indirect or diplomatic in communication.
      • 41-60: Balanced straightforwardness, adjusts communication style based on the situation.
      • 61-80: Moderately straightforward, communicates opinions and ideas clearly and directly.
      • 81-100: Highly straightforward, values direct and honest communication.

  25. Alt (Altruistic Tendencies):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: Less inclined towards altruistic behaviors, more self-focused in actions.
      • 41-60: Balanced approach, exhibits some altruistic tendencies but also considers personal needs.
      • 61-80: Moderately altruistic, actively engages in behaviors that benefit others without expecting anything in return.
      • 81-100: Highly altruistic, consistently prioritizes the welfare and well-being of others.

  26. Comp (Compliance):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: Less compliant, more independent, and less inclined to conform to rules or norms.
      • 41-60: Balanced approach, considers rules and norms but also values personal autonomy.
      • 61-80: Moderately compliant, adheres to rules and social norms but also maintains individuality.
      • 81-100: Highly compliant, respects rules and authority, and places importance on conforming to societal expectations.

  27. Mod (Modesty):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: Less modest, more inclined to self-promotion or exaggeration of achievements.
      • 41-60: Balanced modesty, acknowledges accomplishments but also avoids excessive self-promotion.
      • 61-80: Moderately modest, downplays achievements and avoids excessive self-promotion.
      • 81-100: Highly modest, humble, and avoids drawing excessive attention to accomplishments.

  28. TM (Tolerance for Misbehavior):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: Less tolerant of misbehavior, more prone to reacting strongly to others' mistakes or misbehavior.
      • 41-60: Balanced approach, exercises some level of patience and forgiveness towards others' misbehavior.
      • 61-80: Moderately tolerant, demonstrates patience and forgiveness towards others' mistakes or misbehavior.
      • 81-100: Highly tolerant, exhibits high levels of patience and forgiveness, and maintains a calm approach to others' misbehavior.

  29. N (Neuroticism):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: More emotionally stable, less prone to experiencing negative emotions and mood swings.
      • 41-60: Balanced emotional stability, maintains stability under normal circumstances but may be affected by significant stressors.
      • 61-80: Moderately prone to negative emotions, may experience occasional mood swings or anxiety.
      • 81-100: Highly prone to negative emotions, more likely to experience intense negative emotions and mood fluctuations.

  30. Anx (Anxiety):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: Less anxious, more calm and composed even in stressful situations.
      • 41-60: Balanced anxiety, experiences mild anxiety in certain situations but overall manages stress well.
      • 61-80: Moderately prone to anxiety, may experience frequent worries or nervousness.
      • 81-100: Highly prone to anxiety, often experiences intense worry and nervousness in various situations.

  31. Ang (Anger):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: Less prone to anger, more calm and composed even in frustrating or provoking situations.
      • 41-60: Balanced anger, manages anger effectively, but may experience moderate irritation in certain circumstances.
      • 61-80: Moderately prone to anger, may experience occasional outbursts or irritability.
      • 81-100: Highly prone to anger, more likely to experience frequent outbursts of anger or irritability.

  32. Dep (Depression):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: Less prone to depression, more resilient and maintains a positive outlook.
      • 41-60: Balanced depression, experiences occasional low mood but overall manages emotions well.
      • 61-80: Moderately prone to depression, may experience periods of sadness or hopelessness.
      • 81-100: Highly prone to depression, more likely to experience frequent and intense periods of sadness or hopelessness.

  33. SC (Self-Consciousness):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: Less self-conscious, more confident and less concerned about others' opinions.
      • 41-60: Balanced self-consciousness, aware of others' perceptions but does not excessively worry about judgment.
      • 61-80: Moderately self-conscious, may be more concerned about others' opinions and occasionally feels socially anxious.
      • 81-100: Highly self-conscious, frequently concerned about others' judgments and experiences social anxiety.

  34. Immod (Immoderation):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: More inclined towards moderation and self-control in behavior and habits.
      • 41-60: Balanced approach, exercises moderate self-control but may indulge in some behaviors occasionally.
      • 61-80: Moderately prone to immoderation, may struggle with some impulsive behaviors or overindulgence.
      • 81-100: Highly prone to immoderation, more likely to engage in frequent impulsive behaviors or excessive indulgence.

  35. V (Vulnerability):

    • Score Range:
      • 0-40: Less vulnerable, more emotionally resilient, and less affected by setbacks or criticism.
      • 41-60: Balanced vulnerability, experiences moderate emotional responses to setbacks or criticism but can recover quickly.
      • 61-80: Moderately vulnerable, may be more sensitive to setbacks or criticism, but can manage emotions effectively.
      • 81-100: Highly vulnerable, more emotionally sensitive and may be deeply affected by setbacks or criticism.

These descriptions provide an understanding of how different scores within each dimension reflect varying levels of personality traits and tendencies.
