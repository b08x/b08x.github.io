Abstract

This article presents a novel approach to problem-solving using a collaborative, iterative dialogue-based method. The method involves a team of experts engaging in a structured conversation, where each expert contributes their knowledge and insights to refine and expand upon the ideas of others. The dialogue is guided by a set of predefined commands that ensure thorough understanding and effective tracking of the thought process.

The article demonstrates the application of this method through a specific example, where a team of experts plays a game of twenty questions to identify a problem. The dialogue transcript showcases the iterative nature of the conversation, with each expert asking specific questions, providing insights, and suggesting alternative approaches. The method effectively guides the team towards the correct solution, highlighting the benefits of collaborative problem-solving and structured dialogue.

The article concludes by discussing the potential applications of this method in various domains, such as research, design, and business decision-making. It emphasizes the importance of fostering a collaborative environment where diverse perspectives can be combined to generate innovative solutions.
