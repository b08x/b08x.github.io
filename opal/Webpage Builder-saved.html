Prompt Engineering as a Precision Discipline

Advanced strategies to force the AI to "Think Like a Human" and produce actionable results.

Chain-of-Thought (CoT)

Forcing the AI to decompose, correlate, hypothesize, and remediate. This forensic workflow reduces hallucinations drastically.

1

Step 01: Decomposition

"Break down error 0x000000EF into kernel-level failures."

2

Step 02: Hypothesis

"Rank 3 hypotheses for the root cause."

3

Step 03: Synthesis

"For each hypothesis, propose a verification + fix."

Role Prompting

The "Persona" Hack. Assigning roles shapes output tone and expertise depth.

Senior Systems Architect

"Act as a Senior SysAdmin. Write a script with Try-Catch verbose logging."

Security Engineer

"Review this script for hardcoded credentials or unsafe commands."

Crisis Comm Manager

"Draft an incident report. Tone: empathetic and transparent."
