Concept Analysis

Neurotransmitters

Neurotransmitters are chemical messengers that transmit signals between neurons. Dopamine and serotonin are two neurotransmitters that are involved in motivation. Dopamine is associated with feelings of pleasure and reward, while serotonin is involved in mood regulation.

Herbs and Neurotransmitters

Several herbs have been shown to affect the levels of dopamine and serotonin in the brain. For example, Rhodiola rosea is an herb that has been shown to increase levels of dopamine and serotonin. Ashwagandha is another herb that has been shown to increase levels of dopamine. Panax ginseng is an herb that has been shown to increase levels of serotonin.
