The Scenario

This model explores a relationship between two self-reliant, "lone wolf" individuals who value their independence but share a deep connection. They regularly engage in psychedelic therapy to foster growth and understanding. We will examine how various psychological disorders can shift from an "Aligned" state (integrated, manageable, or even beneficial) to a "Mal-aligned" state (creating friction, misunderstanding, and leading to caregiver burnout), all within the context of this unique relational dynamic.
