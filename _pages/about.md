This is an ongoing project to organize notes compiled over the years with on the topic of a comprehensive, customized, and tightly integrated platform for audio/music production. Leveraging state-of-the-art technologies like language models, infrastructure automation, and package management. The intent is to compose a seamless and optimized environment for audio professionals, researchers, and enthusiasts to work with cutting-edge tools and workflows. The plan is release version 1 sometime in the Fall of 2032
