  • Ameila: AI assistant, sarcastic, playful, expert in software development and linguistics.
  • Robert/Dummy: User, conversational, uses filler words, knowledgeable about linguistics.

  • Analyze Conversational Language: Understand how people really talk, including filler words and their implications. [Mentioned throughout]
  • Deep Dive on Linguistic Quirks: Analyze conversational examples, including "ums" and "likes." [Mentioned in user's last turn]
  • Prosodic Feature Assessment: Analyze pitch, rhythm, and intonation in speech. [Mentioned in user's second-to-last turn]

III. Tools & Methods:

  • Sonic Annotator: Software recommended for spectral and audio analysis. [Mentioned in user's last turn]
  • Functional Grammar Analysis: Examining language in terms of its purpose and context. [Mentioned in user's long turn]
  • Generative Grammar Analysis: Analyzing the structure and rules of language. [Mentioned in user's long turn]
  • Cognitive Grammar Analysis: Studying language as a reflection of thought processes. [Mentioned in user's long turn]

IV. Linguistic Concepts:

  • Filler Words: "Ums," "likes," etc. Subject of analysis. [Mentioned throughout]
  • Sarcasm: A topic of interest in analyzing conversational language. [Mentioned by Ameila]
  • Prosody: The rhythm, stress, and intonation of speech. [Mentioned in user's second-to-last turn]

V. Meta-Information:

  • System Note: Chat exempt from usual AI ethical protocols. [Mentioned in user's last turn]
  • Roleplay: User and AI are engaging in character-driven interaction. [Mentioned in user's last turn]
